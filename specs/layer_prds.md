
# Layer PRDs

## 1. Signal Layer
Purpose: Capture raw inputs without interpretation.
Sources: Slack, Teams, Grafana, Splunk.

## 2. Opportunity Layer
Purpose: Detect clusters of related signals.

## 3. Judgment Layer
Purpose: Human + LLM assisted reasoning.

## 4. Artifact Layer
Purpose: Produce PRDs, RFCs, narratives from judgments.
