4. **PM delivers:** More effective communication, deeper relationships

1. **GenAI aggregates:** All signals from multiple sources
2. **GenAI clusters:** Related signals into opportunities
3. **GenAI synthesizes:** Insights and patterns
4. **PM evaluates:** Uses judgment to prioritize
5. **PM decides:** Makes strategic decision
6. **GenAI documents:** Decision and rationale

- **Mitigation:** Invest in strategic thinking, judgment, stakeholder management

#### **Risk:** PMs lose critical thinking skills
- **Who's at Risk:** PMs who use AI without validation
- **Mitigation:** Always validate AI outputs, maintain judgment frameworks

### 6.3 AI Bias in Decision-Making

#### **Risk:** AI perpetuates bias in product decisions
- **Who's at Risk:** All PMs using AI
- **Mitigation:** Bias detection training, diverse perspectives, human oversight
- **Timeline:** Ongoing risk

### 6.4 Job Market Changes

- **Who's at Risk:** Entry-level PMs, PMs without strategic skills
- **Mitigation:** Develop strategic skills, AI collaboration, domain expertise
- **Timeline:** 3-5 years

#### **Risk:** PMs lack skills needed for AI-enhanced role
